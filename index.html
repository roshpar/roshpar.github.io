About

I bring clarity, structure, and steadiness to fast-moving environments so that creative and marketing teams feel supported to take ambitious leaps with confidence.

My background spans eight years across account leadership, project management, production, and creative operations. My career has taken me across the globe, from multinational agencies to independent creative shops, working agency-side to support creative teams, and client-side to champion brands.

I've led multi-channel and integrated campaigns across digital, social, broadcast, experiential, print, and OOH. From scrappy, just-make-it-happen shoots to big-budget national productions, the kind that win clicks and hearts. I love leading people and process. Not despite the complexity, but because of it.

The best process leaves room for the unexpected. My job is to hold the structure so the team can hold the vision.
